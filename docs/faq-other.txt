Other
=====

Do you offer support?
---------------------

Yes. We offer ticket-based support for all Heroku users through our [zendesk ticket system](http://support.heroku.com), Mon-Fri from 6am-6pm PST. If you need specific response times and SLAs, we offer custom packages. Please contact biz@heroku.com for more info.

I'd like to join your beta program
----------------------------------

Send an email to beta@heroku.com. Be sure to state your acceptance that info furnished through the Heroku beta program will be treated as confidential until otherwise indicated.

Do you have an affiliate program?
---------------------------------

Working on it! Contact us at biz@heroku.com for more info.
